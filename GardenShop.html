Dive Into our Amazing History

Welcome to Our Family Garden where passion for plants meets family values and community spirit. We are more than just a garden store; we are a vibrant, family owned business dedicated to helping you create beautiful, thriving spaces that bring joy to every season. Our goal is to inspire gardeners of all ages and skill levels to cultivate their dream garden while making lasting memories with loved ones.

At Our Family Garden, you will find an array of top-quality plants, seeds, and tools handpicked by our family for yours. We pride ourselves on curating a selection that includes everything from lush houseplants and vibrant annuals to specialty perennials and heirloom vegetables. We are here to support your gardening journey, offering expert advice on plant care, landscaping tips, and seasonal planting strategies. Whether you are starting a backyard vegetable patch, creating a pollinator garden, or just adding a touch of greenery to your home, we have the knowledge and resources to make your vision come to life.

But Our Family Garden is more than a store it is a place where the gardening community gathers. We host regular workshops for families, kids, and garden enthusiasts, covering topics from composting basics to creating sustainable landscapes. Our friendly staff, all experienced gardeners themselves, are always here to answer questions and provide personalized recommendations to suit your space and style.

Visit us to see our seasonal displays, attend an event, or simply find inspiration for your garden. At Our Family Garden, we believe in the magic of nurturing plants and relationships. Whether you are shopping in person or exploring our online store, you are joining a community that values the beauty of nature and the power of family connections. Come grow with us!
